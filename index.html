What is Mental Health to me?

Mental health to me is a state of healthy self consciousness and self image. This particularly comes to play during the times when I am experiencing challenges in my life. If I manage to go through those challenges without distorting the view of myself, then I am a mentally health individual at that point if otherwise, then I am not. I have struggled with my mental health for the longest time. But I have also learned how to pull myself out of the deep sea of sadness, by allowing myself to feel the pain and still remember that it's part of human nature to go through obstacles. Having said this, I am not excusing any other diagnoses people might have, but I am sharing my experience.
